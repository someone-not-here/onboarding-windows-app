## Reflection

### What do you hope to learn from this internship?
I hope to learn what it's like working in a professional development team working on an app, and to learn the various skills required to become a fully fledged developer for Windows.

### What skills are you most excited to develop?
Team collaboration, how to effectively maintain an existing codebase, and various good practices when it comes to coding for a Windows app.

### What do you think you can contribute to the team?
I think I can contribute my attention-to-detail, especially for bugs, as well as figuring out ways to implement new features into the app.

### What is one area you feel less confident in that you’d like to improve?
Integrating AI within applications. AI adds another layer of complexity to a project, and considering Focus Bear uses AI, I would like to better understand how it works and how I can integrate it into projects.

### What does success in this internship look like for you?
Finishing my tasks and objectives, learning a lot from what I do, and contributing something meaningful to the company.
