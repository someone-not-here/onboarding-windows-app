### Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
A lot of productivity apps out don't fully address the specific needs of neurodivergent people. As such, Focus Bear was created with them in mind, specifically designed to support people with ADHD, Autism, and other such disorders or disabilities who have trouble focusing and maintaining routines.

### What problem is Focus Bear solving?
It tries to help people build and maintain routines by blocking distractions from their phones and PCs.

### Why do you think this mission is important?
It's important because most productivity apps out there weren't created with neurodivergent people in mind. With Focus Bear, it was developed not only for them, but also by them, allowing the app to better address the needs of their demographic on a deeper level.

### How does Focus Bear's work align with your personal values or interests?
I enjoy creating applications and have always wanted to help out other people in some way. Working on Focus Bear achieves those goals: I get to help develop the application as well as help a bunch of people along the way.

### Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes. My mind tends to wander whenever I get some downtime when working, often leading me down rabbit holes on the Internet and making me lose focus on my work. It would likely help me to have something to block these distractions and stop my mind from wandering.
